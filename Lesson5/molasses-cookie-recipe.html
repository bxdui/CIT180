Molasses Cookies

Recipe sourced from: https://sallysbakingaddiction.com/soft-molasses-cookies/

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter softened to room temp
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and salt until combined. Set aside.
  2. Mix together the sugar, brown sugar, and softened butter until combined, then add the molasses, egg, and vanilla extract.
  3. Slowly mix the dry and wet ingredients. Once mixed, cover and chill the dough for at least an hour.
  4. Once the dough is chilled, preheat the oven to 350Â°F and line your baking sheet(s) with parchment paper.
  5. If preferred, you can roll the cookies in sugar and wait for them to come to room temperature.
  6. Once the oven is preheated, roll the dough into ~1 inch balls and cook for 12 minutes. Allow cookies to cool for 5 minutes, after which they will be perfectly warm and soft.

Time and Yield

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Yield
~1 hour 45 minutes 12 minutes ~2 hours 30-32 cookies
